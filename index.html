Liked Animates

Fullmetal Alchemist

Very good storyline. There are two versions of it, and I prefer the one released on 2009

Your Name

My favorite animate movie. The scenes in the movie are so beautiful that a lot of frames can be directly used as wall paper. The story is also good.

Demon Slayer

After Naruto and Bleach have all ended, the animate market needs a new main stream adventure story. Demon Slayer is a good candidate. It tells a story about a boy trying to find ways to change his sister, who became a demon, back to human.

Scissor Seven

This is a Chinese animate, but you can find it on Netflix. It tells a story that the best assassin was betrayed by the woman he decided to protect and lost his memory. Now he is a simple and happy barber. However, as more other assassins find him, he starts to recall his memory, and struggles to get rid of his past.

Liked Nintendo Switch Games

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Nintendo use it as the first game on the Nintendo Switch. It is actually really good. The open world game mod gives the player a lot of freedom and the puzzles are fun to solve. Different weapons and fighting methods also attract many RPG players. It is a strong motivation for people to buy a Nintendo Switch.

Hollow Knight

It's a 2D adventure game, but it's really challenging. It's like the 2D version of Dark Souls. This game is developed by a 2 people team from Australia. I really like the art style, and the way they tell the story. It doesn't have a explicit story line, but the player needs to imagine the story based on the conversations with NPCs.

Liked TV Series

Game of Thrones

I finished all the 8 seasons of this TV series. It tells how different families use their strategies to get power and the throne. I don't really like the last season tho. It feels like they just want to end the story. Except the ending, everything else is fantastic.

West World

Great Sci-fi TV series. The last season (season 3) just ended on May. I'm currently on season 2. It tells a story about artificial intelligence human robot got self-awareness and start to fight back. As a technician, I enjoyed watching this show. It drives the audiences to think about where the technology is leading us to.
